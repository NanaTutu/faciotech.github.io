Our approach to hybrid workplace flexibility

At Faciotech, we value and support flexibility as part of our hybrid workplace where every employee can do their best
work by working the way they work best. A hybrid workplace is a mix of workstyles across work site, work location and
work hours.

Work Site

This is the physical place where you work. Work site information provided in a job posting indicates the expectations of working remotely or working on-site. The following information will be listed under the work site field in a job posting:

  • Up to 50% work from home
  • Up to 100% work from home
  • Microsoft on-site only

A job posting that lists 100% work from home can be applied for by anyone located within the listed country.

Work Location

This is the geographic location of your work. Each organization at Microsoft has guidelines related to location flexibility.

“Multiple locations” listed in a job posting means a job can be done from any or several locations within the country listed.

Please make sure to check the work site field to know the expectation of the physical place where you work.

Work Hours

This encompasses the hours and days when you work. We encourage employees to work the hours that are best for them and that allow them to deliver according to expectations.

Work schedules can vary depending on individual needs. You will not see any information around work schedule flexibility in a job posting. Your work hours can be discussed with your recruiter and/or hiring manager during the recruiting process.

Land Management

Faciotech is leading the innovation for managing and securing lands and other real estates using technology. Through its digital platform, it is contributing to addressing the challenge of high vacancy prevalent in most parts of Ghana and bridge the gap between real estate developers and home seekers.
In spite of the huge housing deficit in the country, vacancy rate of buildings remain high. While the situation has in part been attributed to the inability of home seekers to afford available properties, research shows that the daunting task of finding suitable homes from the vast available homes is a significant contributor to the problem.
Despite the increase in the housing stocks in recent times, vacancies rates are still rising with worse housing conditions. Our research indicates a disconnection between real estate developers and home seekers in Ghana. That is, a situation where home developers are either developing for a non-existing market or home seekers are not able to locate their desired properties due to non-availability of data.
We work with real estate businesses to implement our robust yet simple solution that will assist them to reach the ideal homebuyers. We envision the possibility of bridging the disconnect and knowledge gap of the industry.

Technology for Social Causes

We believe in the power of grassroots mobilization and local community causes. That is why we are passionate about supporting groups and organizations to deploy technology in creating awareness on social issues, undertake public sensitization campaigns and mobilize citizens for social causes.
We create online platforms to serve all these purposes including the deployment of crowd funding technology solutions to enable public donations towards a social good.
This solution is particularly important as it can help small and medium scale businesses raise funds to support their enterprises. Beyond donations, the platform also supports exchange of ideas and sharing of stories to encourage users.
Our focus on NGOs ensure that we guide them to maintain a positive online presence and achieve their digital goals. We do this by creating fully functional websites at highly discounted costs to enable them reach wider audiences.
Consultancy services are also available to support NGOs to leverage on the internet to promote their initiatives using dynamic strategies including creative content and is able to drive audience engagement and participation.

Research

Creating innovations to deal with complex issues requires extensive research on various social and economic issues. This is one of our fortes at Faciotech.
Our team of dedicated researchers maintain a high level of discipline and skill in undertaking cutting edge research that presents accurate results critical for making important decisions in the areas of business, economy, agriculture or social undertakings.
Technology plays an important role in this delivery, either internally for our organization’s growth or external for third-party enterprises that hire our service in their quest to come up with viable solutions to solve complex societal issues.
Having empirical evidence helps to create innovations that can be employed to increase efficiency. That is why research is a pivotal to our success at Faciotech.

Contributions to SDGs

he Sustainable Development Goals are a collection of 17 global goals set by the United Nations General Assembly in 2015 for the year 2030. They include No Poverty, Zero Hunger, Good Health and Well-being, Quality Education, Gender Equality, Clean Water and Sanitation, Affordable and Clean Energy, Decent Work and Economic Growth, Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure, Reduced Inequality, Sustainable Cities and Communities, Responsible Consumption and Production, Climate Action, Life Below Water, Life on Land, Peace and Justice Strong Institutions, Partnerships to achieve the Goal.
Our aim at Faciotech is to use technology to work towards achieving all the goals and deliberately share information about the linkages between technology and these goals through symposia and interactive media.
We are also intentional about using our online platforms whip up interest from the public towards contributing their quota towards the achievement of the goals.
