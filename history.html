About Us

Inaugurated in 2023, Faciotech Foundation is a non-profit organization (Reg No. CG037130423), serving as a trailblazer in the realm of information technology infrastructure. With its roots in Ghana, the foundation builds upon the legacy of Facio Innovations Technology, originally established in 2015. Our team, comprised of devoted and seasoned researchers, meticulously conducts thorough research to devise practical and innovative IT solutions. These solutions are designed to stimulate IT literacy, foster social interaction and awareness, and to instigate the development of further innovations adept at addressing societal challenges.

Our mission is to create change socially and economically through IT literacy advancement, research and the creation of online platforms and other innovations that solves dire needs in various areas of the nation. Our work at FacioTech Foundation is guided by the unwavering commitment to catalyze societal advancement and create a significant imprint through the application of technology. In alignment with this commitment, we have developed a multitude of platforms, each designed to foster this cause and make meaningful strides towards our objectives.

Approach

Our approach to this audacious task is to reach out to organizations and individuals, particularly women and young people and use both formal and informal means to train them on innovative technological solutions to common problems and equip them with skills that will allow them to brainstorm and apply technology in their daily lives.

We believe that this approach will result in a ripple effect that will result in a widespread adaptation of technology and enable institutions such as NGOs to amplify their impact.

Our formal training culminates in a valuable certification that can give leverage to young people seeking employment in entry-level IT roles or seeking further education in IT-related courses.

To achieve our modest, yet audacious vision, we have crafted different services that will holistically address the problem of low uptake of technology in rural Ghana. This multi-pronged approach will deliver the highest returns for our work in creating opportunities for IT literacy and impacting lives socially and economically through the power of technology.
