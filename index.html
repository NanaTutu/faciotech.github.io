The Faciotech Foundation is dedicated to creating social change by advancing IT literacy and leveraging technology to provide lasting and viable solutions for complex challenges.
There are several ways you can get involved with Faciotech Foundation. You can donate to support our various initiatives, or you could join us in our mission to create social change. For more details, please visit our 'Donate Today' and 'Join Us' sections on the website.
Ourcauseaid is a crowdfunding platform created by the Faciotech Foundation. It is designed to support small NGOs in Ghana, helping them connect with donors and raise funds for social support initiatives targeting vulnerable and disadvantaged populations. Ourcauseaid exemplifies our commitment to using technology for social causes.
