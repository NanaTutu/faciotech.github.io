Accelerating Mission Outcomes with Technology

At Faciotech, we’re committed to delivering relevant, affordable, and innovative solutions to help nonprofits tackle the world’s biggest challenges. Our vision is to leverage technology to drive greater impact, enabling organizations to achieve their mission outcomes more effectively.

Our Commitment to Nonprofits

We believe in the power of technology to transform the way nonprofits operate, collaborate, and deliver services. Our commitment is to provide nonprofits with the tools and resources they need to succeed in a digital world.

Technology for Social Impact

We offer grants and discounts across our products and services, including cloud-based solutions, data analytics, and digital engagement tools. Our aim is to empower nonprofits to use technology to drive social impact.

Capacity Building

We provide training and learning paths so nonprofit employees can use technology to drive greater impact. Explore our resources and visit our Digital Skills Center to learn more.

Our Partners

We work with partners across the tech ecosystem to deliver solutions and services that drive nonprofit digital transformation. Find a partner that can help you on your digital journey.

Social Investment

At Faciotech, we believe in giving back to the community. We have established a unique social investment model where incremental revenue is reinvested into causes for social good, including education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability.

Why we focus on technology

Technology is not luxury, but a proven catalyst for development and transformation. We believe in the power of technology to break barriers and bridge development gaps by democratizing access to education, employment opportunities, healthcare and information relevant to transform local economies.   That is why at Faciotech, we commit ourselves to advancing IT knowledge and leveraging it to create lasting solutions to common problems faced in all aspects of local economies.
